My Story

I have been developing games since high school, including traditional educational games such as Egrang, Cublak-Cublak Suweng, Gobak Sodor as well as AR (Augmented Reality) disaster mitigation educational games for the "Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa" and casual games protecting spaceships from the threat of meteors for Imagine Cup Games Competition where I am active as a game programmer. Graduated from Gadjah Mada University, Department of Computer Science and Electronics with the thesis topic of Brain Computer Interface for game control systems. I am interested in developing games and their supporting components such as artificial intelligence, as well as automating computer-based tasks such as custom shortcuts.
